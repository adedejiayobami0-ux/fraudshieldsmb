What we catch

The 6 fraud types that
hit small businesses hardest.

These aren't rare edge cases. They happen to businesses like yours every single day.

💳
Card Testing
Fraudsters run small $1–$5 charges to check if a stolen card works, then make a big purchase. FraudShield detects the test pattern before they escalate.
📬
Fake Check & ACH Fraud
Someone pays with a check or bank transfer that bounces 3–5 days later. By then you've already delivered your product or service. We flag these before you ship.
🔄
Chargeback Abuse
Some customers buy, receive the product, then dispute the charge with their bank claiming they never got it. We identify repeat patterns before they cost you money.
🆔
Identity Mismatch
The billing address doesn't match the delivery address. The name doesn't match the card. The device is in a different country. We catch these mismatches in real time.
⚡
Velocity Attacks
Suddenly 30 orders in 10 minutes from new accounts. Or the same card tried 15 times with different amounts. These patterns scream fraud — we alert you immediately.
🌙
Off-Hours Activity
Real customers rarely make large purchases at 3am. FraudShield knows your normal transaction patterns and flags anything that looks out of place for your business.

FAQ

Questions we get
all the time.

Do I need to be a developer to use this?
Not really. If you use Stripe or Square, adding FraudShield is one extra step in your payment flow. We provide copy-paste code for the most common setups. If you get stuck, we'll help you directly.
Doesn't Stripe already handle fraud?
Stripe has basic fraud tools, but they're built to protect Stripe — not you specifically. They flag transactions that are bad for their network. FraudShield is tuned for small business patterns and gives you visibility and control Stripe doesn't offer.
What happens when FraudShield flags a transaction?
You get an instant notification with a plain-English explanation. You decide what to do — review it manually, reach out to the customer, or let the automatic block stand. You're always in control.
Will it slow down my checkout?
No. FraudShield responds in under 100 milliseconds on average — faster than a blink. Your customers will never notice it's there.
What if it blocks a real customer by mistake?
It can happen, and we take it seriously. That's why we show you exactly why something was flagged. During beta, we're actively tuning the model to reduce false positives. You can always override any decision.
Is my customer data safe?
We never store full card numbers or sensitive payment data. We analyze transaction patterns and metadata only. Your data is encrypted and never sold or shared.
