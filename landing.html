What we catch

6 fraud types that hit small
businesses every single day.

💳
Card Testing
Fraudsters run tiny $1–$5 charges to check if a stolen card works, then make a large purchase. We catch the pattern before they escalate.
📬
Fake Check & ACH
A payment that bounces 3–5 days after you've already delivered. By then you've lost both the product and the money. We flag these upfront.
🔄
Chargeback Abuse
Some customers buy, receive the product, then dispute the charge claiming they never got it. We identify repeat offenders before they cost you.
🆔
Identity Mismatch
Billing address doesn't match shipping. Name doesn't match the card. Location is suspicious. We catch these red flags in real time.
⚡
Velocity Attacks
Suddenly 30 orders in 10 minutes from new accounts. Or the same card tried 15 times with different amounts. We alert you the moment it starts.
🌙
Off-Hours Activity
Real customers rarely make large purchases at 3am. FraudShield knows your patterns and flags anything that looks out of the ordinary.

FAQ

Questions we get
all the time.

Do I need to be technical to use this?
Not really. Adding FraudShield is one extra step in your payment flow. We provide copy-paste code for Stripe, Square, and PayPal. If you get stuck, we'll help you directly.
Doesn't Stripe already handle fraud?
Stripe has basic tools, but they protect Stripe — not you specifically. FraudShield is tuned for small business patterns and gives you visibility and control that Stripe doesn't offer.
What happens when a transaction is flagged?
You get an instant notification with a plain-English explanation. You decide what to do — review it, reach out to the customer, or let the automatic block stand. You're always in control.
Will it slow down my checkout?
No. FraudShield responds in under 100 milliseconds on average — faster than a blink. Your customers will never notice it's running.
What if it blocks a real customer?
It can happen, and we take it seriously. We show you exactly why something was flagged so you can override any decision. During beta we're actively tuning to reduce false positives.
Is my customer data safe?
We never store card numbers. We analyze transaction patterns and metadata only. Your data is encrypted, never sold, never shared with third parties.
